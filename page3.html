What We Do

We offer a variety of flexible event packages and work closely with our guests to make each occasion unforgettable.
From small neighborhood dinners and birthdays to corporate events and weddings, we will work with you to craft a unique and singular experience.
In-house catering is available for events of varying sizes. Our farm chefs prepare seasonal, creative & vibrant menus, showcasing vegetables and herbs grown from our farm.

Events & Catering

NEED TO KNOW BEFORE YOUR EVENT

Friends of a farmer offers:
• site visit • rehearsal (for weddings only) • phone and email support prior to the event • staff member onsite for the duration of the event in case of questions or emergencies • guided tour of the sanctuary for event guests
TIMES The rental fee entitles you to use the facilities for six hours, which includes set-up and clean-up time.
FACILITIES Parties can set up and hold their event inside the Barn or rent a tent and put it in the field behind the Barn.
Visitor Barn information:
• building dimensions: 47’ X 46’
• can accommodate 200 guests seated in rounds
• Due to our tour program, events held inside our barn must be planned outside of tour times. Set-up can be as early as 3:15 p.m. and the event/ceremony can begin as early as 5 p.m.
• The Barn is not temperature-controlled, but heaters or fans could be used if rented by the event organizer
Event Field behind the Barn • Large field suitable for any size tent (event organizer is responsible for renting the tent) • Events can be held anytime during the day or evening
FOOD We provide our own chef’s who have amazing menu plans according to your event needs. Outside Caterers will need to provide proof of insurance.
ENTERTAINMENT You are welcome to secure a DJ or band for your event. Any onsite vendors will need to provide proof of insurance or sign a Farm Sanctuary liability waiver. If you are planning to play CDs or use an iPod, you are responsible for securing any licenses to play copyright-protected music, or you may play royalty-free music.
PARTY RENTALS A Friend of A Farmer has tables and seating options available for a flat rental fee of $50. • Up to twelve 5′ wooden benches • Up to twelve 6′ rectangular tables • Up to 30 brown metal folding chairs Any additional equipment or furnishings, such as a tent or tablecloths, must be procured by you.
ACCOMMODATIONS Farm staff has three Bed and Breakfast cabins on the property. Rental of all three cabins for the night of the event is included in the rental fee if the cabins are available. The cabins may be available to rent for additional nights at the regular member rates.
COST The rental fee is a flat rate of $1,500 and includes a guided tour for your guests. A deposit of 50 percent of your total cost is required to reserve the space. The remainder of the balance is due two weeks before the event.

Team Retreats
A great option for groups of small to big groups. For the corporate companies who want to create a wonderful experience by team building in a retreat oasis with amazing food. Mostly know for our "Foodie Forager" package to the "Flexible Farmer" our six itineraries are designed to give your team the perfect balance of work and play. Our team retreat packages are priced per-person and are inclusive of specialized workshops, meeting rooms equipped with av., whiteboards etc., catered rooftop farm lunch and optional meeting facilitation. Our workshop instructors are unparalleled in their fields and you are guaranteed to leave with new skills, broadened horizons, and a feeling of rejuvenation. An experience the whole company will talk about!!!

Capacity: 100 guests
